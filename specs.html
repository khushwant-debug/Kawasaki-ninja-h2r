Technical Breakdown

A focused look at the engineering that makes the Ninja H2R one of the most extreme track-only machines ever built.

Maximum Power 310 PS 326 PS with ram air
Top Speed 400+ km/h Closed-course testing
Engine 998 cc Supercharged inline-four

Engine & Supercharger

At the heart of the Ninja H2R is a hand-built 998 cc supercharged inline-four that delivers explosive power while maintaining incredible smoothness. The engine is designed to work as a complete system with the Kawasaki supercharger, not as an add-on.

  • 998 cc, liquid-cooled, 4-stroke inline-four
  • Kawasaki in-house designed centrifugal supercharger
  • Maximum output: 310 PS (326 PS with ram air)
  • High-performance forged pistons and strengthened components

Performance & Dynamics

Built only for the track, the Ninja H2R is focused on maximum acceleration and stability at very high speeds. Aerodynamic bodywork and downforce elements help keep the bike planted when it matters most.

  • Top speed tested over 400 km/h in closed-course conditions
  • Aerodynamic winglets for increased downforce
  • Race-spec electronics with multiple power and traction modes
  • Single-seat track-only configuration

Chassis & Braking

A lightweight trellis frame, premium suspension and powerful brakes are tuned to manage the massive performance of the supercharged engine.

  • Trellis frame designed for rigidity and feedback
  • High-spec fully adjustable front and rear suspension
  • Racing-grade Brembo braking components
  • High-grip track-focused tyres
  • Long wheelbase stability for extreme speeds
  • Electronics tuned specifically for track use
