About

Built Without Limits

The Ninja H2R is not just a motorcycle. It is Kawasaki’s statement of what is possible when engineering is allowed to ignore the usual restrictions and focus purely on performance.

The Idea Behind the H2R

The Ninja H2R was created as a flagship project, bringing together expertise from multiple Kawasaki divisions, including aerospace and gas turbine engineering. The goal was simple: create a machine that feels like nothing else on a racetrack.

From the distinctive supercharger sound to the aggressive aerodynamic bodywork, every part of the H2R is designed to deliver an experience that is brutally fast yet highly controlled when ridden by skilled hands.

  • Developed by Kawasaki Heavy Industries as a cross-division project
  • Focus on extreme acceleration and high-speed stability
  • Dedicated track-only machine, not homologated for street use

Aerospace Influence

Winglets and body shapes are tuned using know-how taken from Kawasaki’s aerospace division, helping the H2R stay planted at extreme speeds.

Supercharged Identity

The in-house designed supercharger gives the H2R a unique power delivery and sound that sets it apart from conventional litre-class superbikes.

Track-Only Focus

No indicators, no mirrors, no compromises. The H2R strips away road-legal requirements to focus purely on performance on closed circuits.
